Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our student freelance platform.

Who can join Campus Gigs?
Currently, only Nile University students with valid university email address (@nileuniversity.edu.ng) can register as freelancers.
How much does it cost to use the platform?
Campus Gigs is free to join for all Nile University students. We only charge a small service fee on completed projects, which is deducted from the payment before it reaches your account.
How do payments work?
Payments are securely processed through Paystack. Funds are held in escrow until the client approves your work. Once approved, payment is released directly to your bank account or mobile money wallet within 2-3 business days.
What types of jobs are available?
We offer a wide range of freelance opportunities including graphic design, web development, content writing, video production, social media management, data analysis, mobile app development, translation services, and business consulting. New categories are added regularly based on student demand.
